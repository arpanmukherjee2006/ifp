At the Institute of Forest Productivity, our research endeavors are driven by a commitment to sustainable forest management and ecological restoration. Through cutting-edge scientific methodologies and collaborative partnerships, we address critical challenges in forest productivity enhancement, biodiversity conservation, and climate change adaptation. Our multidisciplinary approach integrates traditional knowledge with modern technology to develop innovative solutions that benefit both forest ecosystems and local communities across the eastern region of India.

Research Divisions

Specialized research areas driving innovation in forestry science

Genetics

Advanced genetic research focusing on tree improvement, molecular markers, and conservation genetics for enhanced forest productivity and biodiversity preservation.

Learn More

Forest Ecology

Comprehensive ecosystem studies examining forest dynamics, biodiversity patterns, and ecological processes to support sustainable forest management practices.

Learn More

Silviculture

Innovative silvicultural techniques for plantation establishment, forest regeneration, and sustainable harvesting to maximize forest productivity and ecosystem services.

Extension

Community engagement and knowledge transfer programs connecting research outcomes with stakeholders, farmers, and forest-dependent communities for practical implementation.
